United Meat Market

A branding system for a local business

For nearly 40 years, United Meat Market has been a beloved fixture in Windsor Terrace, offering goods like fresh meat, Italian specialties, and homemade tomato sauce. While customers praise the business' customer service and consistency, United's graphic identity has suffered due to conflicting messages and a lack of time. To address this, I created a memorable system to set United apart. In my redesign, I aimed to maintain United's familiarity. The front's bold, no-nonsense 'MEAT MARKET' signage has made an impact on their customers, despite being a generic typeface. The original card's design also evoked similar feelings, but a redesign was made in 2018. It attempted to do more, but the overuse of images and confrontational typography left the owners unsatisfied. I used two fonts from Heritage Type's Milkstore Font Collection to appease these concerns. They have options for textured glyphs, which reminded me of the materiality of butchering twine and wrapping paper. Combining texture, color, and shape allowed me to create a graphic identity that could stand out on its own.
